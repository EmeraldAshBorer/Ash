for gnosis growing out of you

By Kai Bjork

It is an outgrowth of the Medusa’s head verdant and pending in form and stone-like when drawn to the eye this rupture of spindle filaments will-o’-wisp me away in mystery in my own witnessing of you. Blue jays wail for each other— your own body effervesces for you with a green mass sprouting forth and thinly obelisking outward and valiantly shooting up at the sky persistence synecdoche for you.
