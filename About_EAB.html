Emerald Ash Borer and The Death of Ash

"Emerald Ash Borer, back" by Sam Droege is marked with Public Domain Mark 1.0 .

The Emerald Ash Borer (Agrilus planipennis) is one of the many invasive species that has taken hold in north America. A jewel beetle originating from north-east Asia it has quickly decimated the ash tree population ever since its arrival in Michigan during the 1990s.
